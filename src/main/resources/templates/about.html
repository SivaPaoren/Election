About me

Hello everyone I am Siva, the website owner. This template belongs to © Copyright Philosophy 2018. I am a self-taught developer, I am good with Java programming language and Spring Framework. The backend of this page is written with Spring Boot, Spring Security, Hibernate, MySql, JPA, and the front end template engine is Thymeleaf. The intentions of building this web application are many but one of them is, to enable people of any age and from anywhere around the globe to vote for the 2020USELECTION. I thought of us as a people, why not vote online and see who deserve to be the President of the United State and compare them with actual votes. These are the voices of the whole world. If you come across any bug please let me know. Your data will be deleted after the ELECTION.
